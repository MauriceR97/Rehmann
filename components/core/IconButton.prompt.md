Icon-only button for headers/toolbars. Always pass a `label` for accessibility.

```jsx
<IconButton icon="search" label="Suchen" />
<IconButton icon="shopping-cart" label="Warenkorb" badge={3} />
<IconButton icon="heart" label="Merkliste" variant="subtle" />
```

Variants: `ghost` | `solid` (red) | `subtle`. Shapes `round` | `square`. Sizes keep ≥36/44/52px hit targets.
