Uppercase pill for tags, statuses and the brand's signature red promo flashes.

```jsx
<Badge variant="promo">−30%</Badge>
<Badge variant="navy">NEU</Badge>
<Badge variant="soft">Bestpreis</Badge>
<Badge variant="success">Auf Lager</Badge>
```

Variants: `neutral` | `promo` (red) | `navy` | `success` | `warning` | `soft`. Sizes `sm` | `md` | `lg`. Always uppercase, fully rounded.
